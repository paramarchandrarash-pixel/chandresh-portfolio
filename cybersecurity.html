Stay Safe Online with Simple Security Practices

Why Cybersecurity Matters

Cybersecurity helps protect your personal data, online accounts, banking details, and devices from hackers, scams, and cyber attacks.

1. Use Strong Passwords

Always create strong passwords using uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using your name or birth date.

2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your accounts like Gmail, Instagram, and banking apps.

3. Beware of Phishing

Do not click on unknown links in emails, messages, or WhatsApp. Fake links can steal your password and bank information.

4. Keep Software Updated

Always update your mobile apps, Windows system, and antivirus software to stay protected from new security threats.

5. Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Banking

Public Wi-Fi is not always secure. Do not use it for online banking, payments, or sensitive logins.

6. Use Antivirus Protection

Install trusted antivirus software to detect harmful files, malware, and suspicious activity.

Quick Safety Advice

  • Never share OTP with anyone.
  • Lock your phone and laptop with password or fingerprint.
  • Use different passwords for different accounts.
  • Check website URL before entering sensitive details.
